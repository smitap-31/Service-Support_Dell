Accidents happen. Are you prepared?

We all know accidents happen. Its part of life. Imagine that if when accidents occur to your computers, your productivity does not grind to a halt. We want to help you get back to busi- ness as quickly and efficiently as possible. So we offer Dell Ac- cidental Damage Service a repair and replacement program for select systems and peripherals. This service covers specific damage due to accidents, not covered by your Limited War- ranty.

Dell Accidental Damage covers unintentional damage that results from: liquid spills; electrical surges; and breakage due to drops, falls, or other collisions. In the event that one of your systems is accidentally damaged, you simply contact us direct- ly. After determining the cause and extent of the damage to your system, we will take the appropriate steps to help ensure that it is back in working order as quickly as possible. Services include:

Shipment of customer-replaceable parts

Shipment of the damaged product to a Dell repair facility

Replacement of a product if it has been accidentally dam- aged beyond repair
